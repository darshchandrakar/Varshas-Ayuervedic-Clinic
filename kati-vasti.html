Treatment Process

The Kati Vasti Process

1

Consultation

Personalized assessment to determine back condition and select appropriate oils.

2

Dough Dam

Creating a dough ring barrier on the lumbar region to retain medicated oil.

3

Oil Retention

Pouring warm medicated oil within the dough dam and maintaining it for the therapy duration.
