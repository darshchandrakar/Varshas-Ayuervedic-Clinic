Treatment Process

The Janu Vasti Process

1

Frame Preparation

A leak-proof reservoir is built around the knee joint using black gram dough.

2

Oil Pooling

Warm medicated herbal oil (like Mahanarayan oil) is slowly poured into the reservoir.

3

Temperature Maintenance

Oil is replaced periodically to maintain a therapeutic warm temperature for 30-40 minutes.

4

Gentle Massage

Following the pooling, a light massage is performed to help the joint absorb the nutrients.
