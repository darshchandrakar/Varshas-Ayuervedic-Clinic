Treatment Process

The Natra Tarpanam Process

1

Consultation

Assessment of your dosha type and specific eye concerns to select appropriate ghee.

2

Preparation

Medicated ghee is prepared with herbal ingredients and warmed to optimal temperature.

3

Treatment Application
