## Overview

Professional yet approachable wellness brand with traditional Ayurvedic roots. The design feels trustworthy and calming, with moderate density and a warm, healing tone that balances modern healthcare credibility with ancient wisdom.

Clean, centered layout with generous whitespace. Uses a standard max-width container approach with consistent section spacing. Grid system appears to be based on 16px increments with larger 24px and 100px spacing for major sections.

Uses a 4px base grid with scale: 1, 2, 4, 6, 8, 9.5, 25.

## Colors
- **Healing Teal** (#056256): Header background, primary buttons, brand accent
- **Turmeric Orange** (#fb8e28): Call-to-action elements, highlights, warm accents
- **Pure White** (#ffffff): Main background, card backgrounds, clean surfaces
- **Off White** (#fffffe): Subtle background variation, content areas
- **Sage Gray** (#565555): Body text, secondary content
- **Deep Earth** (#000000): Headings, primary text, high contrast elements
- **Light Gray** (#f9f9f9): Section backgrounds, subtle divisions
- **Medium Gray** (#222222): Secondary headings, medium contrast text

## Typography
- **Headline Font**: Manrope
- **Body Font**: Manrope
- **Label Font**: Public Sans

Typography hierarchy relies on size variation rather than dramatic weight changes, with most text using regular (400) weight and selective use of medium (500) weight for emphasis. The 16px body text scales down to 14px for secondary content, while headings jump to 24px. Line-height follows a 1.5 ratio for body text, creating comfortable reading rhythm. Letter-spacing remains normal throughout, maintaining clean legibility.

## Elevation

Minimal shadow strategy with subtle elevation on interactive elements. Uses soft shadows (rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.15) 0px 2px 6px 0px) sparingly for buttons and cards. Primarily relies on color contrast and spacing rather than dramatic depth effects.

## Components
- **Header Navigation**: Teal background header with white logo, contact information, and horizontal navigation menu with social media icons
- **Hero Section**: Large background image with overlay text, prominent heading, and call-to-action button with rounded corners
- **Contact Info Bar**: Top bar with email and phone contact information, minimal styling with white text on teal background
- **Logo Treatment**: Circular emblem with traditional Ayurvedic imagery combined with modern typography for brand name
- **CTA Button**: Rounded rectangle button with teal background, white text, and subtle shadow for depth

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do use the teal primary color (#056256) for all primary actions and brand elements
- Don't use more than medium (500) font weight - keep typography light and readable
- Do maintain generous whitespace with 24px+ spacing between major sections
- Don't use sharp corners - prefer 5-12px border radius for most elements
- Do ensure sufficient contrast between the dark gray text (#565555) and backgrounds
- Don't overuse the orange accent (#fb8e28) - reserve for key highlights only
- Do use the pill-shaped (9999px) border radius for buttons and badges
- Don't mix too many font families - stick to Manrope for content and Public Sans for navigation
